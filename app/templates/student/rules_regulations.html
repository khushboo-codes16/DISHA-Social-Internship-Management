DISHA Program Rules & Regulations

Guidelines for successful completion of social internship program

General Guidelines

  • All programs must be conducted with proper planning and prior approval
  • Maintain proper decorum and represent DSVV with dignity
  • Follow all safety protocols during program execution
  • Respect local customs and traditions of the community

Program Conduct

  • Submit program reports within 7 days of completion
  • Document programs with photographs and participant details
  • Maintain accurate records of participants and outcomes
  • Submit complete program documentation for approval

Code of Conduct

  • Strictly no political or commercial activities during programs
  • No discrimination based on caste, religion, or gender
  • Zero tolerance for substance abuse during programs
  • No unauthorized use of DSVV name or logo

Assessment Criteria

Program Quality (40%)

  • • Planning and preparation
  • • Execution quality
  • • Documentation
  • • Impact assessment

Community Impact (30%)

  • • Participant engagement
  • • Community feedback
  • • Sustainable outcomes
  • • Social relevance

Teamwork (20%)

  • • Collaboration
  • • Leadership
  • • Coordination
  • • Peer evaluation

Innovation (10%)

  • • Creative approach
  • • Unique solutions
  • • Replicability
  • • Best practices
