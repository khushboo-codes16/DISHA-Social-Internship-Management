Official Guidelines Document

General Guidelines

  • Program Planning: All social service programs must be conducted with proper planning and prior approval from the DISHA committee.
  • Professional Conduct: Maintain proper decorum and represent Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya with dignity and respect at all times.
  • Safety Protocols: Follow all safety protocols and guidelines during program execution in various communities.
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Respect local customs, traditions, and cultural practices of the communities you serve.

Program Conduct & Documentation

  • Timely Submission: Submit detailed program reports within 7 days of program completion through the online portal.
  • Documentation: Document all programs with photographs, participant details, and impact assessment data.
  • Record Keeping: Maintain accurate records of participants, outcomes, and community feedback for assessment.
  • Complete Documentation: Submit comprehensive program documentation including attendance sheets and achievement reports.

Code of Conduct

  • No Political Activities: Strictly no political or commercial activities during DISHA programs. Focus solely on social service.
  • Zero Discrimination: No discrimination based on caste, religion, gender, or socioeconomic status in program implementation.
  • Substance Abuse: Zero tolerance for substance abuse during programs. Maintain clean and healthy lifestyle.
  • Brand Usage: No unauthorized use of DSVV name, logo, or branding for personal or commercial purposes.
